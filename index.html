About

Hello, I’m Daquon, a dedicated web developer with a focus on crafting dynamic and responsive web applications. With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks such as React and Node.js, I aim to create smooth, user-centered digital experiences. My process blends effective problem-solving with writing clean, maintainable code. I enjoy tackling challenges and am always excited to explore new technologies. When I’m not coding, you’ll often find me building intricate LEGO sets—it's a bit of an obsession—or in the sky, working on my private pilot’s license. Whether on the ground or in the air, I’m always looking for new challenges and adventures. Let’s collaborate and create something great—take a look at my work or reach out to connect!
